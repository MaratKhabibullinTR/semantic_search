Explosion at Kentucky UPS facility injures 2 employees

May 30 (Reuters) - Two United Parcel Service Inc employees were injured in an explosion on Wednesday at one of the package delivery company’s freight facilities in Lexington, Kentucky, a UPS spokesman said.
UPS said a gas leak from propane tanks inside a trailer may have caused the blast at the facility.
Reporting by Eric M. Johnson in Seattle Editing by Chizu Nomiyama
